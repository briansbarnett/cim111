History

Electronic Music has it's origins in Europe -- having been popularized in exotic destinations such as Ibiza, Spain. This hot new genre made it's way over to Detroit, MI where "The Warehouse" became an epicenter for the first forms of "house music". The sound evolved over time and now, electronic music has hundreds of sub-genres.
