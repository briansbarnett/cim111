How To Make a Box of Brownies

Step 1

Buying the Box

I prefer Ghiardelli Brownie Mix. I've found it to be the most moist and has the best cake to chip ratio.

Step 2

Prepping the Ingredients and Pre-Heating

Empty the box onto your counter. Gather a mixing bowl, spoon or spatula, and all required ingredients. In this case, 2 eggs, vegetable oil and water. Pre-heat the oven as the package dictates.

Step 3

Mix Well

Use your spoon or spatula to well incorporate all of the ingredients. Be sure there is no dry mix left visible.

Step 4

Pouring the Mix

Make sure you grease your brownie pan before pouring the mix. Once poured, hit the pan a few times against the tabletop to get rid of air bubbles.

Step 5

Serve and Enjoy!

Allow brownies to cool slightly before serving. Cut into 9 equal squares and serve!
