TOM NORRIS


Tom Norris is a multi-platinum award winning producer, mix and mastering engineer. He began working with electronic duo Grey in 2015 - mixing and mastering the 3x Platinum hit Starving featuring Hailee Steinfeld and Zedd. He continues to work with Grey, having mixed and mastered their debut EP Chameleon, their collaboration with Camila Caballo entitled Crown for the Netflix original movie Bright and the international smash The Middle with Zedd and Maren Morris, which has garnered more than 750 million streams globally.

Norris and Skrillex began to work together on a number of projects including the Skrillex remix of Kendrick Lamar's Humble, the Skrillex remix of Ekali's Babylon, the Skrillex remix of Pendulum's The Island Pt. 1 and From First To Last's latest single Surrender. The two continue to work together on a daily basis, where Norris serves as an in-house sonic creative, mixer and mastering engineer.

Other notable projects include those for U2, Cheat Codes, Ansel Elgort, BloodPop and The Weeknd.
